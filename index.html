About KrishEdge

Built from the fields of Pathanamthitta

KrishEdge was born out of a simple observation — Indian farmers are skilled, hard-working, and underserved by technology. We're changing that with ground-up, hyperlocal tools that work in every language and on every phone.

  • Hyperlocal trust-building in rural Kerala
  • Partnered with Krishi Bhavans and NABARD
  • Supported by KSUM and YIP 8.0
  • Works in all 22 scheduled Indian languages
  • No smartphone required — WhatsApp & IVR support
